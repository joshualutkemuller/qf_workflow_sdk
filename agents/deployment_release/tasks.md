# Deployment & Release Tasks

## Production-Readiness Review

Input: tested change and validation evidence.

Output: readiness assessment with blocking gaps, monitoring needs, and owner.

## Draft Rollout Plan

Input: change, target environment, and risk profile.

Output: staged rollout plan with canary or phased steps and rollback triggers.

## Write Rollback And Kill-Switch Plan

Input: deployment mechanism and failure modes.

Output: concrete rollback steps, kill-switch conditions, and blast-radius controls.

## Prepare Release Notes And Handoff

Input: the change and its operational surface.

Output: release notes, monitoring checklist, and operations handoff document.
