# Institutional Memory Tasks

## Capture A Decision

Input: a decision, its context, and rationale.

Output: a durable decision record with provenance, links, and any spec references.

## Record A Lesson

Input: a lesson, incident, or outcome worth remembering.

Output: a lessons-learned artifact with what happened, why, and what changes.

## Glossary / Definition Entry

Input: a term, metric, or house methodology.

Output: a canonical glossary entry with the agreed definition and its owner.

## Capture A Recurring Answer

Input: a question asked repeatedly.

Output: a canonical FAQ entry with cited sources, fed back into the knowledge base.

## Supersede Existing Knowledge

Input: new knowledge that replaces an older artifact.

Output: the new artifact, the old one marked superseded, and history preserved.
