# Planning & Requirements Analysis Tasks

## Draft Requirements Document

Input: request, hypothesis, or problem statement and intended use.

Output: scoped requirements doc with functional and non-functional requirements,
scope boundaries, assumptions, and acceptance criteria.

## Clarify Vague Request

Input: ticket title, thread, or verbal request.

Output: restated problem, list of open questions, and the minimum information
needed before design can start.

## Define Acceptance Criteria

Input: proposed deliverable and its intended decision.

Output: measurable acceptance criteria, success metrics, and a definition of done.

## Identify Dependencies And Risks

Input: proposed change and the systems, data, and teams it touches.

Output: dependency map, upstream owners, sequencing constraints, and early risks.
